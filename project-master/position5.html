    • Alumni Advisor

    The position of alumni advisor is important in ensuring that Barsema's Closet has continuous success after its founders have left NIU. It is crucial to ensure that the current student management team is going down the right path and following Barsema's Closet original mission. The alumni advisors act as a point of contact to the current management team in case they have any questions or run into issues. The students will report bi-weekly or as needed to the alumni advisors

    Responsibilities

      Work alongside the management team. Will include weekly/bi-weekly calls and email communication
      Ensure the team is upholding the mission of Barsema's Closet and meeting the monthly objectives
      Collaborate with NIU College of Business to continue a close partnership with alumni and develop strategic innovations
      Advise the current management team on any questions or concerns they may have
      Available to commit at least 5 hours per week

    Qualifications

      Worked on the Barsema's Closet management team as a student
      Demonstrates a strong passion for the business and its mission
      Advanced communication skills
      Leadership experience
      Good at giving appropriate advice
