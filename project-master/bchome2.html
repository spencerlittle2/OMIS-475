What We Do:

We provide all Northern Illinois University students with affordable professional clothes, so they feel prepared and confident going to professional events such as interviews, networking events, and job fairs.

How We Do It

We use a donation-based business model that allows faculty, staff, alumni, business partners, and students to donate gently worn or new professional clothes and accessories. All donations are available to NIU students to purchase at a very affordable price.
