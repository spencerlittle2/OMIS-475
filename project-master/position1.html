    • Executive Manager

    The position of the executive manager is to manage Barsema's Closet to ensure that the operations are running smoothly. They work closely with the rest of the management team, faculty advisors, and alumni advisors. It is their responsibility to keep the whole team on track with the goals and mission of Barsema's Closet. They also will be the main point of contact for donors, faculty, and customers who have questions about the store.

    Responsibilities

      Oversee the management team to ensure members are upholding the mission of Barsema's Closet and meeting the monthly objectives
      Run the weekly meetings
      Collaborate with faculty, staff, business partners, and alumni to develop strategic innovations
      Answer emails directed to the Barsema's Closet email account
      Meet with the faculty advisor frequently to ensure success of the store
      Update the alumni advisors on the status of the store
      Work with management team to develop a succession plan
      Available to commit 15-20 hours per week during the 2019-2020 academic year

    Qualifications

      Strong writing and communication skills
      Good email etiquette
      Leadership experience
      Exceptional organizational skills and high attention to detail
      Passionate about supporting other students and their professional development
      Preferred but not required: Interest in non-profit or retail management
      Completed or currently enrolled in the business core courses
