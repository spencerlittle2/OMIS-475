    • How to Donate

    Barsema's Closet functions due to your charitable donations. We would greatly appreciate donations of professionalwear and/or your time!


    Donations

    Donations are mailed or dropped off to the College of Business Advancement Office (Barsema Hall room 145)at 740 Garden Rd, Dekalb IL 60115


    Volunteering

    students are encouraged to volunteer at Barsema's Closet! Once a week, donation volunteers sort and organize donations. Volunteers are also needed to help run the store on shopping days. All volunteers receive service passport credit and 50% off thier next purchase at the store!

    If you are interested in volunteering, please contact the team here!
