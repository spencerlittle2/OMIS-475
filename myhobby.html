Spencer's love story for hockey

My interest for ice hockey started at a young age. My parents taught me how to ice skate when I was around 5 years old. While growing up it was common for my siblings and I to ice skate on frozen winter ponds or at the local park district. My interest for ice skating grew into a passion for the Chicago Blackhawks.
