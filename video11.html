Great Gatsby Movie Trailer and Review

The new Great Gatsby Movie was released on May 15, 2013. This movie is based on F. Scott Fitzgerald's novel from 1925. This movie was directed by Baz Luhrmann and the main character was Leonardo DiCaprio. This movie was filmed in Australia and had a production budget of $105 million. The new Great Gatsby Movie has grossed over $353 million dollars.
The Great Gatsby is a story told by Nick Carraway who was one of Gatsby's neighbors. The story takes place on Long Island during the infamous Roaring Twenties. This movie shows the glamorous lifestyle of the "New" upper class and their exquisite parties.
