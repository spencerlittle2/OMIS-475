University Background

About Northern Illinois University:

Northern Illinois Univeristy is a public research university founded in 1895 that is located in DeKalb, IL. The unviersity is a suburban setting and sits on 800 acres and is composed of 13,454 undergraduate students. The university is located approximately 60 miles west of Chicago.

About the College of Business:

The College of Business at Northern Illinois Univeristy is the only school in Illinois to be ranked number 1 by Businessweek for providing the greatest return on investment. The College of Business is composed of 5 undergraduate departments and contains 8 Graduate programs.
