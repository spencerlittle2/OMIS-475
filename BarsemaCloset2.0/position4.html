    • Marketing Manager

    The position of the public relations manager is to manage all of the social media accounts for Barsema's Closet. The image of Barsema's Closet on social media is very important since that is where all our donors and customers learn more about us. The PR manager must ensure that we maintain a positive reputation on social media and that the pages are easy and fun to navigate. It is important to keep customers and donors engaged with the successes of Barsema's Closet.

    Responsibilities

      Post regularly on social media accounts including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn
      Respond to all direct messages on social media accounts
      Create aesthetically pleasing marketing materials including flyers, posters, signs, and online postings
      Maintain a positive and personable image on all social media accounts
      Take pictures frequently of store happenings
      Develop outreach tactics to engage NIU faculty, students, alumni, business partners, and other donors
      May assist executive manager in responding to email inquiries
      Available to commit 10-15 hours per week during the 2019-2020 academic year

    Qualifications

      Strong writing and communication skills
      Friendly and positive attitude
      Excellent team player
      Creative skills
      Knowledge of social media outlets and marketing software such as Canva
      Completed or currently enrolled in the business core courses
