    • Human Resource Manager

    The position of the volunteer coordinator is to manage all things related to volunteers. They will schedule the volunteer events, recruit volunteers, and manage volunteers during events. This position is very important to maintain the image of Barsema's Closet with the volunteers and ensure that the mission is being upheld by all volunteers. The volunteer coordinator should be available for each store opening.

    Responsibilities

      Schedule volunteer events
      Recruit and train volunteers
      Send out reminders on the volunteering events
      Manage volunteers during events
      Work closely with the passport office
      Meet with the rest of the management team to assist in reaching goals
      Available to commit 10-15 hours per week during the 2019-2020 academic year

    Qualifications

      Enjoys working with people
      Experience or interest in managing individuals
      Strong organizational skills
      Ability to influence and persuade others
