    • Finance Manager

    The position of the finance manager is to manage the funds of Barsema's Closet. Since we have an active revenue stream, the finance manager is responsible for collecting those funds and allocating them accordingly. The person in this position is also responsible for creating budgets, managing monetary donations, and maintaining the transaction programs. This person will work closely with the NIU Foundation and other staff members at NIU in order to ensure Barsema's Closet is running smoothly and according to its mission.

    Responsibilities

      Create a budget per semester
      Run the weekly meetings
      Manage the monetary donations, transaction applications, and petty cash
      Ensure that all accounting and financial rules are being met through operations
      Communicate with the NIU Foundation and College of Business gift officer to manage donations, store revenue, and store expenses.

    Qualifications

      Trustworthy and responsible
      Strong organizational skills
      Knowledge in the accounting and finance fields
      Experience using transaction recording applications such as Square Register and Paypal
      Effective math skills
      Good team player
      Completed of currently enrolled in the business core courses
